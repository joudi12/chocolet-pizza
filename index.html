For the fig-swirl: Melt butter over medium heat in a saucepan. Add sugar Halve all of the figs and toss in the saucepan with water and lemon juice. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until you have mixture. Add salt with one or two stirs, set aside and let cool

Ice cream: In a small pot over medium heat, combine milk, and granulated sugar until sugar is completely dissolved and just barely lukewarm. Whisk in the egg yolks. Set mixture in the fridge and wait until the fig mixture is cooled.

machine, pour liquids into the frozen basin and process according to manufacturer instructions, i.e., let spin and thicken for 20 minutes before adding mascarpone, fig jam mixture, and the nuts. Continue to process for +/- 10 minutes. Pour semi-frozen mixture into a pyrex dish or glass tupperware. Freeze for at least two hours before serving.
